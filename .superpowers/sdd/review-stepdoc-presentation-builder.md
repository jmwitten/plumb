# Armchair caddy naive-builder review

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-13
**Document:** regenerated five-panel assembly manual
**Reviewer context:** competent handyman without contracting or engineering
training; compared against public Article and IKEA side-table manuals.
**Initial verdict:** buildable with minor guessing, not yet guess-free.

## Parts and order

The reviewer understood the intended object and chronology and found every
modeled member and screw: five wood members and eight screws. The two-rail
ordinal wording resolved which rail pairs with which side board.

## Reader-facing gaps

1. The side-board top/end/front/back/rail witness faces were implicit in the
   picture rather than stated.
2. The rail's 3/4-inch station did not name the face being located.
3. Adhesive appeared only at the bond operation, not in the pre-build
   inventory.
4. Nominal lumber names were presented as if they were actual finished
   dimensions.
5. The generic screw omitted the explicit builder-selection boundary for its
   maker/model and drilling requirements.
6. The cup bore used radius/full-cylinder language instead of ordinary diameter
   shop language and did not say the cutter remains unselected.
7. The four station sentences did not identify which rail each pattern belongs
   to.

The review also requested a pre-glue sofa fit check. That is not adopted in
this increment because it would add an unmodeled operation and contradict the
declared bench-then-set boundary. The correction instead exposes the compiled
rail opening and preserves the explicit compatibility/insertion-travel limit.

## Limits that must remain honest

Structural capacity, stability, sliding resistance, insertion travel, and
hot-drink use are unproved. No working-load or universal sofa-fit claim may be
added without representative product selection, prototype testing, and the
appropriate analysis.
